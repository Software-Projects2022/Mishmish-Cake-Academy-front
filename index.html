What I do

Rachelles specialises in creating gorgeous bespoke cakes for weddings, parties and special celebrations. Every cake is unique, exquisite and custom designed.

I work closely with every client thriving on my personal, friendly and attentive service.

From consultation to delivery I aim to make your experience as stress free as possible.

Taste is as important as style which is why only the finest and freshest ingredients are used.

Your cake will be a feast for the eyes and the palate too. I'm based in West Dorset with delivery available to surrounding counties or on request.

I also specialise in the art of floral piping and cake decorating with private master classes held in my studio and online tutorials on offer from my Cake School!
